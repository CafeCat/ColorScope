What Color Bee dose...
With Color Bee, you can collect and store colors you like for your projects. Color Bee provides you different ways of collecting colors, testing colors you have selected for your palettes. If you need more than just watching the value of colors in your palette, just export your palette to .bmp file, and sample colros from it in the painting program you use. Color Bee wish to inspire you, help you make the colors not just delight but personification.
